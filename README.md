## 💚 What is MindEase?

MindEase is a comprehensive mental health platform designed specifically for healthcare professionals, offering tools for stress management, burnout prevention, and peer support.

### ✨ Key Features

- 📊 **Wellness Dashboard** - Track your mental health metrics
- 🧘 **Guided Meditations** - Professionally curated for medical professionals
- 👥 **Peer Support Network** - Connect with colleagues securely
- 📝 **Journaling Tools** - Reflect on challenging cases
- 🚨 **Crisis Resources** - Immediate access to mental health resources
- 🔒 **HIPAA Compliant** - Secure and confidential

## 🎯 Target Users

- Physicians & Residents
- Nurses & Nurse Practitioners
- Medical Students
- Healthcare Administrators
- Allied Health Professionals
